About The Project

A Center for Worship and Community

Bells University Mosque will be more than just a place of prayer. It will serve as a hub for the Muslim community, offering:

  • Daily prayers and Jumu'ah congregation
  • Quran classes for children and adults
  • Islamic lectures and seminars
  • Islamic services and community events
  • Charitable activities and food distribution

The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "Whoever builds a mosque for Allah, Allah will build for him a house in Paradise." (Bukhari & Muslim)
